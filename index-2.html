  • History About Foursquare

    The Foursquare Church, founded by Aimee Semple McPherson in 1923 in Los Angeles, California, emphasizes a "foursquare" gospel message centered on Jesus Christ as Savior, Healer, Baptizer with the Holy Spirit, and soon-coming King.

  • Foursquare Nigeria

    Foursquare Gospel Church in Nigeria was established in Nigeria in 1955 by missionaries from the United States, led by Reverend and Mrs. R.A. Babalola. The Foursquare Church in Nigeria has since grown to become one of the largest Pentecostal denominations in the country, with a strong presence and influence in the Nigerian Christian community.
